Eligibility & Documents

Eligibility

  • Minimum age: 21 years (varies by lender)
  • Maximum age at loan maturity typically 65-70 years
  • Stable income and a good credit score (650+ recommended)

Common Documents

  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport)
  • Address Proof (Utility bill/rental agreement)
  • Income Proof (salary slips, bank statements, ITR)
  • Property Documents (sale deed, title documents, NOC)

Loan Types

  • Home Purchase Loan
  • Home Construction Loan
  • Balance Transfer
  • Top-up Loans
